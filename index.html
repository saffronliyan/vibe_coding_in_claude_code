AI Through the Ages

Key milestones that shaped the field of artificial intelligence.

1950

The Turing Test

Alan Turing proposes a test of machine intelligence: can a computer fool a human into thinking it is one?

1956

"Artificial Intelligence" Coined

John McCarthy organises the Dartmouth Workshop, officially naming the field and launching academic AI research.

1997

Deep Blue Defeats Kasparov

IBM's chess-playing computer beats the reigning world champion, marking a milestone in AI strategic reasoning.

2012

Deep Learning Breakthrough

AlexNet wins ImageNet by a huge margin, proving deep convolutional networks can outperform hand-crafted vision systems.

2022

ChatGPT Launches

OpenAI's conversational AI reaches 100 million users in two months, bringing large language models into everyday life.
